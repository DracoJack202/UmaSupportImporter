Extension for Chrome, Opera GX or anything really.
Just import the extension.

1. Go to Gametora Support Card Comparison section or to this place to be exact https://gametora.com/umamusume/compare
2. Click on the Script, click on Copiar CSV
3. A pop up says that the CSV-text has been copied on your clipboard
4. Paste it on the excel on the page that says Import Support
5. Copy the table on the right to SortedData and now you have more cards to compare from. Enjoy!
